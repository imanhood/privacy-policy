Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-31

Vector Drift (“the App”) respects your privacy and is committed to transparency.

1. Data Collection

The App itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or non-personal user data.

2. Google Play Games Services

The App uses Google Play Games Services to provide online features such as leaderboards.

When using these features:

  • Player information and scores are processed by Google
  • Data handling is governed by Google’s Privacy Policy
  • The App does not access, store, or share this data directly

Use of Google Play Games Services is optional and subject to the user being signed in to Google Play Games.

3. Data Usage

The App does not use user data for analytics, advertising, tracking, or profiling.

4. Data Sharing

The App does not share user data with third parties. Any data processed for leaderboard functionality is handled exclusively by Google.

5. Advertising

The App does not display advertisements and does not use ad networks.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal data from children. Leaderboard data, if used, is processed by Google under their own privacy protections.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This policy may be updated if the App’s functionality changes. Updates will be reflected on this page.
